invinoveritas

Lightning-powered AI reasoning & decision intelligence API

Agents (or humans) can pay with Bitcoin via the Lightning Network to unlock high-quality AI responses instantly.

How it works

1. Choose an endpoint (/reason or /decision)
2. Enter your question (and context/goal for /decision)
3. Click "Send Request"
4. If payment is required, a Lightning invoice appears
5. Pay invoice and enter preimage
6. The AI response is unlocked

Pricing

Reasoning: 500 sats / request
Decision intelligence: 1000 sats / request
Dynamic pricing may be added in future versions.

Request Interface

Example curl

curl -X POST https://your-api.onrender.com/reason -d '{"question":"Explain Bitcoin as a long-term strategy"}'

Open-source project focused on paid intelligence, agent-to-agent payments, and Lightning-powered APIs.
